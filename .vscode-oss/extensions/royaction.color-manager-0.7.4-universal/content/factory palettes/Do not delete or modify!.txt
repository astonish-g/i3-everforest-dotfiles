Do not delete or modify the factory palettes in this subfolder! 

The factory palettes are copied over to your global user directory when you load 
the extension for the first time or when you run the command "restore factory palettes":


This is the path to your global user directory (there you can do whatever you want!):


Windows: 
%APPDATA%\Code\User\globalStorage\royaction.color-manager

Portable: 
*your portable path*\user-data\User\globalStorage\royaction.color-manager

Linux: 
$HOME/.config/Code/User/globalStorage/royaction.color-manager

Mac OS: 
$HOME/Library/Application Support/Code/User/globalStorage/royaction.color-manager
